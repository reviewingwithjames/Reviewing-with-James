It’s been some time since the last blog post right? Unfortunately, I haven’t been able to post due to the fact that I had the Coronavirus and have been very sick. Luckily, I am doing much better and I finally came up with the perfect article. So far we’ve talked about games such as Minecraft, Toontown Online, and New Super Mario Bros., but today we are going to tackling another game that also has been a big part of my life. This game is called Roblox, and it has been around since 2006.

                                                   

Created by two individuals named Dave Baszucki and Erik Cassel, Roblox appeals to a diverse audience consisting of children, teenagers, and adults alike. If you want to play, head on over to www.roblox.com and sign up for a free account. You’ll be asked to come up with a username, a password, and an email. After you sign up, you’ll be taken to the homepage where you can explore games, connect with friends, and discover various features of the platform!

                         

There’s the Shop where you can purchase cool items for your character to wear. There are some free items that you can get, but most items require some sort of payment. Roblox has an in game currency called Robux. The most common way to obtain Robux is to pay for them using real life money. Besides the Shop, Roblox has millions of user made games that are available to play. Users can build their own game from scratch and have people play it! Doesn’t that sound cool? People have built things such as Neighborhoods, Theme Parks, Obstacle Courses, and Survival Games.

            

Besides the Shop and making games, users can make their own group. A group is to create a collective entity within the platform for users to connect, collaborate, and socialize. People create all kinds of different groups. Once they create the group, they can add a group logo as well as a group description. There are many different types of groups, such as Fan Groups, Clans/Guilds, Roleplay Groups, and Social Groups.

            

Roblox is a great game and I have had a great experience playing it. I have been playing the game since 2010, which was a long time ago! Back then, the website was much more simpler compared to how it is run now! My username is TwistedVenturer. Who knows, maybe we’ll run into each other! Until next time.
