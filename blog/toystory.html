Toy Story: Unlocking the Magic of Imagination | 2/12/24

                         

To Infinity and Beyond! What’s good everyone and welcome back to another exciting blog post. Today’s topic covers Disney’s Pixar’s Toy Story.For those of you that don’t know, Toy Story is a beloved animated film produced by Pixar Animation Studios, and the first computer animated Pixar film.

                                                   

So Toy Story takes place in many areas, but the first location and probably the main location is Andy’s House. Andy is one of the main characters, he’s around 6 or 7 years old. Andy lives at home with his mother and sister Molly. Andy has a bunch of different toys that come to life when he is not in the room. They can walk around and act like us humans do. Examples of toys that he has include: Sheriff Woody, Mr. Potato Head, Rex, and Slinky.

                         

Andy is having a birthday party and the toys were very nervous because this means that Andy would potentially get new toys which means new friends. During the birthday party, Andy received many gifts, one of them being a Buzz Lightyear action figure. Buzz Lightyear is a space ranger. When Andy’s mother called the kids at the birthday party downstairs for cake and ice cream, the toys came to life, and so did Buzz. The toys loved him, except Woody. Woody was Andy’s favorite toy, and he was very jealous of Buzz Lightyear. He thought he was going to get replaced. The problem with Buzz Lightyear was that he thought he was a real space ranger, the toys believed him but Woody was not impressed. He was trying to convince everyone that he’s just a toy, but no one would listen

            

This conflict between Woody and Buzz lasts for a majority of the movie. Later on, Sid, Andy’s next-door neighbor, who is around the same age as Andy, obtains Buzz and Woody and wants to destroy them. You see, Sid destroys toys for fun. He’s seen as the villain in this movie. The two toys are forced to team up as a result of this, and they had help from the other toys which Sid had previously tried to destroy. In the end, Buzz and Woody safely return back to Andy, but now the two are best friends.

            

This is probably the best ever movie released by Pixar. It is very entertaining and I loved it growing up. I personally recommend you see this movie, you will enjoy it!
